Privacy Policy – A Season of Joy

This page applies to A Season of Joy. For the app landing page, see A Season of Joy.

Last updated: 25 Jan 2026

Joy is developed and operated by KYP Studio AS (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Overview

Joy is designed as a personal and calm space for everyday joy.

The app does not require account creation and is built to function with minimal data handling.

When enabled by you, Joy uses Apple CloudKit to synchronize your data securely across your own Apple devices.

2. Data you create in the app

Joy allows you to create personal content such as notes and preferred activities.

This content is created solely for your personal use and is stored locally on your device and, if iCloud synchronization is enabled, securely synced to your private iCloud account using Apple CloudKit.

We do not access, read, analyze, or otherwise process this content.

3. CloudKit and iCloud synchronization

Joy uses Apple CloudKit to keep your content in sync across devices signed in with your Apple ID.

Key points:

Synced data is stored in your private CloudKit database

The data is associated with your Apple ID, not with KYP Studio AS

We do not have access to your Apple ID or iCloud account

Apple provides the CloudKit infrastructure under its own privacy terms

You can enable or disable iCloud synchronization at any time through your device or iCloud settings.

4. Limited technical data

To operate the app reliably, we handle a very limited amount of technical information, such as:

app configuration required for basic functionality

subscription status (if applicable), handled by Apple via In-App Purchases

We do not:

collect account credentials

collect location data

use tracking for advertising

sell or share your data for marketing purposes

5. Third-party services

Joy relies on Apple’s standard services, including:

App Store distribution

In-App Purchases and subscriptions

CloudKit for data synchronization

These services are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies.

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

6. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

Where applicable, limited technical data is handled based on:

Your consent

Performance of a contract (providing app functionality)

Legitimate interests, such as maintaining and improving the app

Personal content stored locally or in your iCloud account is not processed by KYP Studio AS.

7. Data retention

Your personal content remains on your device and, if iCloud synchronization is enabled, in your private iCloud account.

We do not access or store copies of your personal content on our own servers.

8. Your rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have rights related to personal data.

However, because Joy does not provide accounts and KYP Studio AS does not access or store personal content, these rights are generally exercised directly by you through the app and your device or iCloud settings.

This includes viewing, editing, or deleting your content, disabling synchronization, or removing the app at any time.

9. Children’s privacy

Joy is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Joy does not provide accounts or user registration, and KYP Studio AS does not collect personal content from users of any age.

Any personal content created in the app is stored locally on the user’s device and, if enabled, in the user’s private iCloud account.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected in the app or on this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, data protection, or the app in general, please contact us at:
