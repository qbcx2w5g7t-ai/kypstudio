About the app

Days can get busy.

They are often driven by things we have to do – meetings, obligations, and to-do lists that never seem to end.

In the middle of that, it is easy to fall into the trap of letting go of the things that bring you joy.

A Season of Joy is created in recognition of the importance and power of instead adding in activities that you want to do – activities you genuinely look forward to.

A joy activity can be anything – big or small – as long as it makes you smile, sparks anticipation, or simply feels good to think about.

The app supports you by helping ensure that you have at least one thing to look forward to each day.

Not as a task.

Not as a goal to optimize.

But as a gentle reminder to listen to what you feel like doing.

Small moments of joy, experienced often, can quietly change how life feels.

This app is here to support you in creating these moments ✨
